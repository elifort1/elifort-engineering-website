Specialized Engineering & Construction

Delivering precision, safety, and excellence across a full spectrum of engineering disciplines in East Africa.

Phase 01

Design & Consultancy

The blueprint for success starts with expert analysis and innovative planning by EliFort Engineering Limited.

Architectural Design

We provide innovative architectural design services, translating client visions into functional, aesthetic, and sustainable structures. Our process integrates creative space planning with rigorous adherence to building codes.

Structural Design & Analysis

Our expert structural engineers deliver precise designs that guarantee the safety, stability, and longevity of your structure. We utilize advanced software for load calculation and stress analysis.

Geotechnical Investigations

Our geotechnical services provide critical subsurface analysis for project foundations. We conduct soil boring and testing to deliver accurate reports on soil bearing capacity and stability.

Land & Topographical Surveying

Using advanced technology like Total Stations and GPS, we deliver high-accuracy land surveys. This precise data is essential for site planning, boundary demarcation, and earthwork calculations.

Phase 02

Core Construction & Management

Executing complex projects with EliFort Engineering Limited's rigorous quality control and safety protocols.

General Construction

We offer expert general construction services for residential, commercial, and industrial projects. Our approach emphasizes quality craftsmanship and strict safety protocols (HSE).

Construction Project Management

Our dedicated teams oversee all phases of construction, from procurement to final handover. We act as your single point of contact, ensuring adherence to budget and timeline constraints.

Electrical & Data Installation

We offer expert electrical system design and installation, ensuring safe, reliable, and efficient power distribution compliant with all modern regulatory codes.

Plumbing & Sanitary Works

EliFort Engineering Limited provides expert plumbing services, designing and installing reliable, high-quality water supply and drainage systems for all project types.

Phase 03

Specialized Finishing & Interiors

The final touch that defines the quality and occupant experience of an EliFort Engineering Limited landmark.

Interior Design & Fit-Out

Our interior services create spaces that are functional, ergonomic, and aesthetically aligned. We manage the entire fit-out process, from space planning to bespoke furniture selection.

Waterproofing & Dampness Control

We specialize in diagnosing and treating water leakages. Utilizing proven waterproofing systems and crystalline coatings, we resolve issues at the source to protect structural integrity.
