From Blueprint to Landmark

Our portfolio is a testament to our engineering philosophy: every project, regardless of scale, is an opportunity to deliver lasting value, precision, and strength.

UMA Commercial Space - Office Fit-outs

UMA, Kampala

Expertise: Aluminium Partitions, Space Optimization, Acoustic Separation, Office Fit-outs.

Engineering Insight: Modern office fit-outs require a balance of aesthetics, flexibility, and performance. For this project, we deployed a modular aluminum partitioning system chosen for its high recycled content and adaptability. Our technical focus was on achieving a specified Sound Transmission Class (STC) rating for Acoustic Separation, crucial for confidential meeting rooms. This was accomplished by using laminated acoustic glass and integrating neoprene seals within the frame channels. The Space Optimization was achieved through a collaborative design process, ensuring the layout supported the client's workflow while maximizing natural light penetration throughout the floor plate.

DFCU Fort Portal - Interior Refinishing

Fort Portal, Uganda

Expertise: Interior Painting, Suspended Ceiling Works, Professional Finishing.

Engineering Insight: Achieving a Professional Finish in a high-traffic corporate environment like a bank is a science. Our process began with a thorough surface analysis, leading to a multi-stage preparation process to achieve a Level 5 finish—the highest industry standard for smoothness. For the Interior Painting, we specified a high-performance, low-VOC (Volatile Organic Compound) scuff-resistant paint to ensure longevity and maintain indoor air quality. The Suspended Ceiling Works involved the precise installation of an acoustic mineral fiber tile system, selected not only for its clean look but also for its high Noise Reduction Coefficient (NRC), significantly improving the acoustic comfort within the banking hall.

Commercial Office - Custom Gypsum Ceilings

Nakasero, Kampala

Expertise: Gypsum Works, Custom Ceiling Design, Electrical Installation.

How We Do It: Beyond aesthetics, this gypsum ceiling was engineered as a functional architectural element. The structure was precisely framed to conceal HVAC ducting and electrical conduits, demonstrating our ability to merge complex M&E requirements with sophisticated interior design for a clean, modern, and highly professional finish.

Kisaasi Apartment - Modern Shower Cabinets

Kisaasi, Kampala

Residential - Modern Floor Tiling

Kampala, Uganda

Expertise: Tiling Works, Substrate Preparation, Large Format Tile Installation.

How We Do It: Large format tiling demands precision. We began by ensuring the concrete substrate was perfectly level using a self-leveling compound. We then employed a tile leveling system with clips and wedges to eliminate lippage, creating a monolithic, seamless surface. Our choice of a flexible, polymer-modified thin-set mortar ensures a superior bond and accommodates micro-movements in the structure, preventing future cracks.

How We Do It: This project began with a client's vision, which we translated into a fully realized digital model using Building Information Modeling (BIM).
